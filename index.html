About

CoCo Chocolatier was created by Kevin "CoCo" Konrad in 2011 in Orlando, Florida. It started out as just a Fruit and Nut chocolate company, but then grew out and made over the top unique flavours. These flavours all have unique names to make it a more fun experience. All the cocoa that goes into the chocolates are organic and the healthiest you can find. CoCo's do online deliveries and also have ouput stores across the world.
